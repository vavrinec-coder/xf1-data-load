XF1 Data Load Support

This Excel add-in now uses the XF1 cloud backend for Zoho authentication, accounting sync, and cached value lookups.

  • Open the add-in pane from Excel.
  • Save your Cloud Identity with your work email.
  • Click Connect Zoho to authorize your own Zoho Books account.
  • Click Sync Accounting Data to refresh the cloud cache for your connected company.
  • Use =XF1.ACC_VAL(account, period) in Excel.
