XF1 Data Load Support

This Excel add-in relies on the XF1 local desktop companion for Zoho authentication, accounting sync, and cached value lookups.

  • Open the add-in pane from Excel.
  • Click Connect Zoho to authorize your own Zoho Books account.
  • Click Sync Accounting Data to refresh the local cache.
  • Use =XF1.ACC_VAL(account, period) in Excel.
